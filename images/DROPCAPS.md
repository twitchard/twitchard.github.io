# Drop cap alphabet

`dropcap-a.jpg` … `dropcap-z.jpg` are a complete A–Z set of decorative
drop-cap initials used to open `class: prose` posts. See the `drop-cap` skill
for how they are applied.

## Source and attribution

These are cropped details from **"The Landscape Alphabet"** — a series of 26
lithographs in which each capital letter is formed from a landscape scene.

- Designed after **L. E. M. Jones**, published c. 1830s.
- Held by the **British Museum**, museum number `1870,0514.2075-2100`.
- **Public domain** (the work is long out of copyright; the British Museum
  scans are released as public domain).
- Sourced from **Wikimedia Commons**:
  https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:The_Landscape_Alphabet

Each file here is a single plate from that series, auto-cropped to the
letterform. The plates were matched to letters using the British Museum
catalogue description of each scene (e.g. "Letter S: composed of two sailing
boats … carried to the right by a wave").
